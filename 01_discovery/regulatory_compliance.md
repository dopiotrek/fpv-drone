# Regulatory Compliance Strategy

## Regulatory Category (EASA)
**Chosen Category:** [A1 / A3]

### Requirements for 5-inch (A3)
- [ ] Pilot Registration (Operator ID).
- [ ] A1/A3 Online Training & Exam.
- [ ] Maintain 150m distance from residential/commercial/industrial areas.
- [ ] Maintain 150m distance from uninvolved persons.

### Requirements for Nano <250g (A1)
- [ ] Pilot Registration (Operator ID) - Only if equipped with camera.
- [ ] No minimum distance from people (just "do not overfly crowds").
